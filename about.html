About Us

Rathi's Group Of Construction Co. is a leading construction consultancy service provider with a wide range of services comprising Quantity Surveying, Cost Management, Project Management and Building Information Modeling. Rathi's Group is serving its Clients in India & Forigen contry. Our rich experience of working with Global Clientele and passion to deliver excellence stand us apart in the industry. We strive to give only unbiased and correct advice to our clients, help our clients save time and money, optimize our clients return on investments whilst making our clients working lives easier and minimize their risk. In addition of being diligent and professional in the services we deliver, we are credited for the way we deliver it aided by some pro-active and profound stakeholder management. Our people are passionate to deliver a world class customer centric service to our clients which has helped us to be at the forefront of our profession, setting industry standards and continually improving our service offering. We have now achieved a status of being a “Company of Brand”.
