About Rathi's Group Of Construction Co.

  • Jan 9, 2016 /

Rathi's Group Of Construction Co. has been the mastermind for scores of head-turning projects for more than a century. This company has ranked among the leading general builders in the India since the early 2016's. Rathi's provides construction and project management services for commercial and multifamily buildings, as well as correctional, educational, entertainment, and manufacturing facilities. The company is also a leader in sustainable or green building practices. Founded in 2016 by Rathi's Group Of Construction Co., the company is the main operating unit of The Rath's Corporation, which is a subsidiary of Indian construction group Rathi construction.
